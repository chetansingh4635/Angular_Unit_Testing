Email support@jclttime.com or call us any time at 1-800-272-2707.

What happens if I close the application before saving my changes?

Unsaved changes will not be saved. Note that users are logged out automatically after 30 minutes of inactivity. Upon automatic logout, any unsaved changes will be lost.

Time Review

How do I review a submitted time sheet?

Login and you will see your Pending Timesheets. Click on the Edit button of the timesheet you would like to review for a detailed summary of the timesheet by day.

What happens after I approve a time sheet?

After approving a Contractor's time sheet, a notification will be sent to the Service Coordinator at the booking agency. The Service Coordinator will then review the now-approved time sheet and electronically enter it in their billing/pay system.

Note that once a time sheet has been approved in the application, it cannot be altered. If any changes need to be made after it has been approved, please contact your assigned Service Coordinator.

If any questions or issues come up regarding a time sheet, please direct them to your assigned Service Coordinator.

What happens after I decline a time sheet?

After approving a Contractor's time sheet, a notification will be sent to the Service Coordinator at the booking agency. The Service Coordinator will then review the now-approved time sheet and electronically enter it in their billing/pay system. Note that once a time sheet has been approved in the application, it cannot be altered. If any changes need to be made after it has been approved, please contact your assigned Service Coordinator. If any questions or issues come up regarding a time sheet, please direct them to your assigned Service Coordinator.

How should I report an issue with a time sheet?

If you happen to find something wrong on a time sheet, please contact your assigned Service Coordinator. In this version of the application, once a Contractor submits their time sheet in the application, it can no longer be edited electronically. Editing of previously submitted/approved/declined time sheets will be available in a future release.

Note that overtime is calculated separately from these electronic time sheets. Overtime will not display in this version of the application.

How do I edit a pending time sheet?

In the BETA version of the application, once a Contractor submits their time sheet in the application, it can no longer be edited electronically. Editing of previously submitted/approved/declined time sheets will be available in a future release.. If an edit is required, please contact your assigned Service Coordinator who will work with the contractor to have a paper time sheet filled out for the correlating work week.

I cannot find a Contractor's time sheet. What should I do?

If you believe that you are missing a time sheet, please contact your assigned Service Coordinator who will work to understand why the booking and time sheet are not displaying in the application for you.
